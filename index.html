Welcome to CDUS

Your secure, peer-to-peer system that unifies all your personal devices into a single logical environment.

Connect Your Devices

Sync occurs directly between your devices over local networks (or fallback secure relay) using encrypted connections.

Go to the Devices tab, click Scan for Devices, or scan a QR code to connect your laptop, desktop, or mobile phone.
